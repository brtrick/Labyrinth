Instructions

  • Setup

    • 16 pieces, each with 4 attributes:
      • Color (dark or light)
      • Size (tall or short)
      • Solidity (hollow or solid)
      • Shape (box or cylinder)
    • Pieces begin the game off the board

  • Objective

    • Create a row/col/diag of 4 pieces that all have at least one attribute in common.
    • For example, 4 pieces that are all tall.
    • The catch? Your opponent chooses the piece you will play (and vice versa).

  • Game Play

    • Play begins when Player 2 selects a piece for Player 1 to place on the board.
    • Once a player has placed a piece on the board, they then select the piece for their opponent to place.
    • Players continue alternating turns.
    • The game ends when either:
      1. A player forms a winning row/col/diag of 4 pieces
      2. All pieces have been played

  • Game Mechanics

    • To select a piece for your opponent, click on a remaining piece.
    • To place a piece on the board, click one of the small circles on the board.
    • To rotate or tilt the board / piece to be played, press down on the mouse button and move the mouse in the desired direction.
    • Note: clicking a board circle to move the board will ALSO place a selected piece.
